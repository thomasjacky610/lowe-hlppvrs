Lowe's Home Improvement: America's Trusted Store

Established in 1921 and headquartered in Mooresville, North Carolina, Lowe's has become one of the most recognized and trusted names in home improvement retail across the United States.

With thousands of stores nationwide and a powerful online platform, Lowe's serves both everyday homeowners and professional contractors — offering everything from tools and appliances to garden supplies and building materials.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Lowe's survey and prize draw

What is the Lowe's Customer Satisfaction Survey? +

The Lowe's Customer Satisfaction Survey is an official feedback program that allows customers to share their shopping experience. Lowe's uses this feedback to improve their stores, staff training, and product range. Participants who complete the survey are entered into a monthly draw to win a $500 Lowe's gift card.

Do I need a receipt to take the survey? +

Yes, you will need a valid Lowe's purchase receipt to access the survey. The receipt contains the store number, date of purchase, and transaction details needed to verify your visit before the survey begins.

Is it free to enter the $500 prize draw? +

Yes, the survey and prize draw entry are completely free. No purchase is required to participate. Simply complete the survey honestly and submit your contact information to be entered into the monthly $500 gift card draw.

How long does the survey take? +

The survey typically takes between 5 and 10 minutes to complete. You'll be asked to rate aspects of your shopping visit such as staff helpfulness, product availability, store cleanliness, and overall satisfaction.

Are there discounts available at Lowe's? +

Yes! Lowe's regularly runs seasonal sales, weekly deals, and promotional events. You can find the latest discounts on the Lowe's website or mobile app. Joining the MyLowe's Rewards programme also gives you access to exclusive member-only offers and cash back on purchases.

Is Lowe's shopping available online? +

Yes, Lowe's has a full online store at Lowes.com where you can browse, compare, and purchase products for home delivery or in-store pickup. The Lowe's mobile app also lets you check product availability at specific store locations before you visit.
